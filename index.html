Philip Joseph "P.J." O'Connor

September 16, 1943 — March 13, 2026

Salem, Massachusetts — Hendersonville, North Carolina

Philip Joseph O'Connor lived across decades and roles — athlete, student, executive, coach, father, grandfather. From the streets of Salem, Massachusetts, to a distinguished career in aerospace and defense, his life was shaped by ambition, loyalty, and an unwavering love of competition. This site honors his memory and invites you to remember him.
